Frequently Asked Questions

Help with the system

How do I start voting?

Click the Vote Now button on the home screen and enter your registry full name and index number to pull up your empty ballot.

Can I change my vote after submitting?

No, once you click submit and clear the confirmation step, it locks completely. Review your choices carefully.

What does "Abstain" mean?

Abstaining means choosing not to choose any of the running candidates for that single spot. It is skipped safely.

Is my vote secret?

Yes, totally anonymous. The system registers that your Index Number voted, but breaks your choices away into a blind dynamic tally.
